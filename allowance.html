Allowance - N 12,750,000

The sum of 12.7 million naira is given to each senator every month to cover a list of allowances. They include

1. Hardship allowance

2. Constituency allowance

3. Furniture allowance

4. Newspaper allowance

5. Wardrobe allowance

6. Recess allowance

7. Accommodation

8. Utilities

9. Domestic staff

10. Entertainment

11. Personal assistant

12. Vehicle maintenance allowance

13. Leave allowance

14. Severance gratuity

15. Motor vehicle allowance
